Manhole Covers

Manholes are openings to a space that we don't usually recognise or consider in our day to day lives. Beneath us lies a vast network, making connections between us and countless other systems such as the water, sewerage, telephone, electric, heating and gas services. The designs on these manhole covers often indicate what system they are connected to.

WCC

Wellington City Council stormwater manhole cover with a floral design. Stormwater is the water that runs off surfaces such as roads, driveways, footpaths and rooftops. The Wellington stormwater network is made up of 650km of pipes and 20km of tunnels, demonstrating just how vast this system is.
