Telecommunications

Telecommunication is the transmission of information via wire, radio, optical or other electromagnetic systems through the use of technology. Telecommunication networks have played an important role throughout history, and have allowed us to continue developing our technology. They provide us with power, internet, phone lines among other things and have a huge impact on the way we live.
